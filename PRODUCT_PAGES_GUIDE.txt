NEXTGEN SMART FINANCE MANAGER (FINYX)
PRODUCT GUIDE - DETAILED PAGE-BY-PAGE EXPLANATION

==================================================
1) PRODUCT OVERVIEW
==================================================
Finyx is a personal finance planning app designed to help users control day-to-day spending and improve long-term financial outcomes.

The product is built around five practical outcomes:
- Know exactly where money is coming from and going.
- Stay within category budgets and avoid overspending.
- Build savings habits through goal tracking and scenario planning.
- Get intelligent guidance through insights, health scoring, and assistant support.
- Make better tax decisions through India-focused deduction and regime comparisons.

Finyx supports both individual users and platform administrators.
- Individual users manage their own money, goals, reports, and preferences.
- Admin users manage platform operations, user roles, and audit visibility.


==================================================
2) GLOBAL PRODUCT EXPERIENCE (ACROSS APP PAGES)
==================================================
A) Sidebar navigation
- Acts as the primary movement system across the app.
- Groups pages into: Overview, Manage, Analytics, and Tools.
- Helps users switch from operational tasks (transactions, budgets) to strategic tasks (reports, insights, tax).

B) Top bar controls
- Shows the current page title and relevant quick actions.
- Provides app-level personalization controls such as theme and font size.
- Displays contextual status elements (date, plan badge, action buttons depending on page).

C) Persistent account context
- User identity, plan tier, and profile context are visible and consistently available.
- Role-based page visibility ensures users only see relevant experiences.

D) Notification and confirmation flow
- The app uses clear success/warning/error feedback after major actions.
- Users receive immediate confirmation for create/update/delete/import actions.

E) Product tour
- A guided walkthrough explains major pages and key value areas.
- Useful for first-time users and for rediscovering advanced features.

F) In-app AI assistant
- Available inside the app as a contextual chatbot.
- Answers app-related questions such as: where to perform actions, how to optimize spending, how to interpret insights, and what to do next.
- Uses user context (plan, activity, spending patterns) to make responses more relevant.

4) CROSS-PAGE USER JOURNEY (PRACTICAL FLOW)
==================================================
Typical end-user journey in Finyx:
1. Discover value on Landing page and create account.
2. Complete onboarding personalization.
3. Add/import transactions and set up accounts.
4. Create budgets and savings goals.
5. Track subscriptions and recurring obligations.
6. Use dashboard daily for quick status checks.
7. Use reports, insights, health score, and simulator weekly/monthly for optimization.
8. Use tax center for periodic tax planning.
9. Use profile/settings to manage identity, preferences, and exports.


==================================================
5) PRODUCT POSITIONING SUMMARY
==================================================
Finyx is a complete personal finance operating system for users who want practical control over cashflow, spending behavior, recurring obligations, savings progress, and tax planning.

The product combines:
- Operational control (transactions, budgets, subscriptions, accounts)
- Strategic planning (goals, reports, simulator, tax center)
- Intelligent assistance (AI insights, health score, in-app assistant)

Result for users:
- Better visibility
- Faster decisions
- Stronger money habits
- Higher confidence in financial planning
